About

At One14 Juice Bar, we believe in keeping things simple, fresh, and full of flavor. What started as a small idea to make healthy choices more accessible turned into a local favorite for smoothies, cold-pressed juices, and wholesome bowls. Every blend we create is made with real ingredients—no shortcuts, no artificial stuff—just pure fuel for your body and mind. Whether you’re grabbing a quick post-class pick-me-up or hanging out with friends, One14 is where good energy meets great taste.
